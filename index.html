AVSET-10M: An Open Large-Scale Audio-Visual Dataset with High Correspondence

Abstract

Groundbreaking research from initiatives such as ChatGPT and Sora has underscored the crucial role of large-scale data in advancing generative and comprehension tasks. However, the absence of comprehensive, large-scale audio-visual datasets with high correspondence has posed a significant challenge in fields such as video dubbing and audio-visual retrieval. To address this gap, we introduce AVSET-10M, a high-correspondence audio-visual dataset comprising 10 million samples, offering these fundamental features: (1) Audio-Visual Correspondence: Through meticulous data filtering, we ensure robust correspondence between the audio and visual components of each dataset entry. (2) Large Scale: With 10 million samples, AVSET-10M is the largest publicly available audio-visual correspondence dataset. (3) Comprehensive Categories: Encompassing 527 unique audio categories, AVSET-10M offers the most extensive range of audio categories available. We have benchmarked two critical tasks on AVSET-10M: audio-visual retrieval and visual query-based sound separation. These tasks highlight the essential role of precise audio-visual correspondence in advancing multimedia research.
